Frequently Asked Questions

Q How Do I Enter To Stand A Chance To Be Part Of The Nedbank Ke Yona Team?

A Entries open on 16 February 2015. To enter, simply dial *120*539662# (*120*KEYONA#) on your mobile phone and follow the prompts. You'll be asked a few simple questions about yourself to be eligible for selection.


Q I Dialled *120*Keyona# But I Could Not Enter?

A Entries open on 16 February 2015. Please try again then and kick-start your dreams. If the problem persists after entries have opened, it might be due to network coverage or congestion in your area. We suggest that you try again after a few minutes.


Q I Do Not Have A Mobile Phone. How Can I Enter?

A Unfortunately you need to enter via mobile phone. We suggest you try and borrow one from a family or friend. Remember that notifications will be sent to the number you entered from so make sure you ask the person who lent you the phone to share the messages with you.


Q What Type Of Phone Do I Need To Enter?

A You may enter using any mobile phone. You don't need the internet and you don't need a smartphone. Entries can be made on Vodacom, MTN, Cell C or Telkom Mobile. Currently, participation on the Virgin Mobile network is still being confirmed.


Q How Much Does It Cost To Enter?

A Entries cost 20c for every 20 seconds. Once you've dialled *120*539662#, you'll be asked a few simple questions about yourself to be eligible for selection. This should not take long at all. Remember that entries open on 16 February 2015.


Q Can I Enter Multiple Times?

A Only one entry per person is needed. Repeated entries will not increase your chances of selection.


Q What Skills Do I Need To Be Part Of The Nedbank Ke Yona Team?

A Passion and talent. The Nedbank Ke Yona Team will be selected by our judging panel based on their soccer skills. So the better you are, the better your chances.


Q Once I Register, What Do I Do Next?

A Lucky entrants will be selected from 1 April to 11 May depending on which tryout venue they've selected, and will be advised that they are through to the next round via SMS. They will be prompted to send us their ID numbers and will then be invited to their nearest stadium to attend tryouts. Those who have not made the selection will also be notified via SMS.


Q How Will People Be Selected For The Nedbank Ke Yona Team?

A To be eligible for selection, you must register via your mobile phone by dialling *120*539662#. Players will be selected according to certain criteria in our Terms and Conditions. For example, you must be a Nedbank client between the ages of 18 and 25 years old and must be a South African male citizen. We're looking for players from all South African provinces and all team positions. If you qualify, you'll be entered into a draw in your province where a certain number of people will be invited to the tryouts. Professional soccer judges will assess your skills and select the top contenders for the final round of tryouts from which the final team will be chosen.
