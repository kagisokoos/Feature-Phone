Full terms and conditions

Nedbank, as a proud sponsor of the Nedbank Cup, is creating a unique opportunity for everyday South Africans to do something extraordinary – become soccer superstars! Everyday people with a talent and love for soccer can register for tryouts and stand a chance to be selected as a member of the Nedbank Ke Yona team.

There will not be a cash reward for the Nedbank Ke Yona team members. The prize consists of a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to compete as a member of the Nedbank Ke Yona team against the 2015 Nedbank Cup champions and to stand a chance to be scouted by one of the professional soccer clubs in South Africa ('the Prize').

  1. The promoter of the Competition is Nedbank Limited (Reg. No 1951/000009/06) ('the Promoter').
  2. This Competition commences on 16 February 2015 and closes on 24 May 2015 ('the Competition Period').
  3. Competition random draw dates to select players for the respective tryouts are as follows:
    1. Mbombela Stadium, Nelspruit – 1 April 2015
    2. Free State Stadium, Bloemfontein – 7 April 2015
    3. Nelson Mandela Bay Stadium, Port Elizabeth – 10 April 2015
    4. King Zwelithini Stadium, Durban – 17 April 2015
    5. Athlone Stadium, Cape Town – 5 May 2015
    6. Orlando Stadium, Johannesburg – 11 May 2015
  4. The Competition is closed to the Promoter's staff, directors, agents, consultants, contractors or any other person who directly or indirectly controls or is controlled by the Promoter or a supplier in connection with the Competition.
  5. The final squad of players who participated in the 2013 and 2014 Nedbank Ke Yona team Challenge against Kaizer Chiefs and Orlando Pirates respectively will also not be eligible to enter the Competition.
  6. To be eligible to enter the Competition participants must be:
    • New or existing Nedbank clients;
    • between the ages of 18 and 25;
    • male South African citizens with valid South African identity documents; and
    • non-professional soccer players.
  7. To enter participants must dial *120*539662# (*120*KEYONA#) from a cellphone and follow the prompts.
  8. Participants must answer a few simple questions about themselves to be eligible for selection, and verify whether or not they are new or existing Nedbank clients.
  9. Any type of cellphone can be used to enter and no connection to the internet is required.
  10. The registration call will cost R0,20 per 20 seconds.
  11. The Competition is only open to those calling from MTN, Vodacom, Cell C and Telkom Mobile networks.
  12. The only mode of entry is through a cellphone and all further notifications will be sent to the number of the cellphone used for entry
  13. Only one entry per person is needed and repeated entries will not increase chances of selection.
  14. Successful participants will be selected by means of a random computer algorithm from 1 April 2015 and will be advised by the dedicated Nedbank Ke Yona team call centre if they are through to the next round. An SMS confirmation with a unique registration code will also be sent to successful participants reminding them of the tryout venue information.
  15. Unsuccessful participants will also be notified by SMS.
  16. Participants will receive an SMS confirmation of their tryout date, followed by a call to confirm match times on the specific date of tryouts at their stadium. Participants who are not able to make it must call 0861 547 856 before the tryout date to speak to a call centre agent who will capture the details of the participant's call. The call centre is only open to people who have been selected for the tryouts, and standard network rates apply.
  17. A panel of judges will select the Nedbank Ke Yona team members based on their soccer skills. The judges' decision will be final and no correspondence will be entered into.
  18. There will be four phases to this Competition:
    • registration and identification of soccer talent around the country through six regional tryouts;
    • selection of a squad of 48 players, who will then be put through various training camps;
    • announcement of a final squad of 16 players to form the Nedbank Ke Yona team; and
    • preparation and training of the Nedbank Ke Yona team as professionals ultimately to take on the best when they meet the winners of the 2015 Nedbank Cup.
  19. The tryouts will take place from 18 April 2015 to 30 May 2015 in six provinces across South Africa
  20. The Promoter will not be responsible for supplying soccer kits or soccer boots to any of the participants nor will it be liable for any costs in this regard.
  21. The Promoter will launch a reality TV series to show viewers behind-the-scenes footage of the tryouts.
  22. The TV series will be aired on SABC 1 (broadcast schedule to be confirmed).
  23. The Nedbank Ke Yona team will be announced on SABC 1 (date to be confirmed)
  24. The exact date when the Nedbank Ke Yona team will face the 2015 Nedbank Cup champions during 2015 will be announced at a later stage.
  25. All logistics costs related to the selected Nedbank Ke Yona team members, such as meals, accommodation and transport to and from the various training camps will be paid for by the Promoter.
